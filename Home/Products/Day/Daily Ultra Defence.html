Daily Ultra Defence
dry / mature skin

A super rich formulation, which protects from the harsh drying, ageing effects of the sun. A carefully chosen complex combining selected and complimentary plants from the sea, rich in polysaccharides, sorbitol, amino acids, essential fatty acids, carotenoids, Vitamins A, B, C, E, minerals, provides significant hydration. Hyaluronic acid, one of the most important moisture holding substances, adds significantly to this new technologically advanced formulation. Skin is deeply moisturised, with improved smoothing and a more youthful healthy glow.

Directions for use

Allow a minute prior to applying make-up. When exposed to extreme sunshine reapply as necessary. Use in conjunction with other RegimA home care.
