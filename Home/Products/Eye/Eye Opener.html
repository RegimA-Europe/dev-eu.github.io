Directions for use

AM & PM: Cleanse with RégimA and apply the Eye Opener serum to the whole eye area.  Ideally combined with New Expression 365 Under Eye Fix.  If so, one may then apply a small amount of Under Eye Fix to the whole under eye area, crow's feet and between the eyebrows on frown lines. Because there is a cumulative effect with these 2 RégimA Zone eye products, if applied twice daily one receives extra benefit. 
The combination of RégimA Eye Opener and New Expression-365 Under Eye Fix is highly recommended.
