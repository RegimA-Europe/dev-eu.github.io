Zone - Pigment Perfector
combats pigmentation

Pigment Perfector boasts an almost entirely new range of actives. Utilising the newest and most cutting edge technologies available for the elimination and prevention of hyperpigmentation.
Whitening agents such as Kojic acid can potentially damage healthy cells, Whereas the lightening compounds in this formulation provide the same level of anti-pigmentation action, without harming viable cells, providing much greater safety with the same efficacy.
The lightening ingredients in this formulation have several separate effects to achieve superior anti-pigmentation results: skin lightening (tyrosinase  inhibition, reduced melanin synthesis), skin brightening (smoothed skin surface and even tone), a flash effect for radiance (increased light reflection from improved skin micro-relief), and an increased skin luminosity effect (optically activated particles).

Directions for use

Following cleansing am and pm, apply eye care products, apply a thin layer of Pigment Perfector to full face if there is widespread pigmentation (do not apply only to patches). If there is pigmentation on the upper or lower eyelids one may apply am and pm replacing upper eyelid care. To be used in conjunction with other RgimA products. To be followed by a RgimA day product containing UVA & UVB protection to help prevent recurrence of pigmentation.
